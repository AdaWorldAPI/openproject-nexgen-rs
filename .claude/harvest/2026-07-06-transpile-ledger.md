## Classid scheme (V3 NodeGuid/Facet тАФ `8:8:16`)
Each `CompiledClass.facet` (16-byte `ruff_spo_address::Facet`) carries the V3 render classid:
```
u32 = [ domain:8 ][ appid:8 ][ classview:16 ]     canon HIGH / custom LOW
        тФФ classid  тФФ app       тФФ up to 65536 ClassView ids per program
                                  (currently the 0x1000 V3-generation placeholder)
```
Human-readable mnemonic `0x07:01::1000`. OpenProject is one `appid`; the `classview:16`
space is the per-app render/ClassView addressing (the layer after this SHAPE emit).
(NOT the stale `[concept:16][app:16]` of OGAR's older `render_classid`.)
